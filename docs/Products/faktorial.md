# Faktorial

![Faktorial home page](/img/products/faktorial-home.png)

Faktorial is Asynkron's autonomous engineering delivery product for teams that manage work in GitHub. It operates the delivery loop inside a repository: investigate an issue, build the change, prove it with tests and CI, prepare the pull request, and capture lessons for the next run.

:::tip Product links
- Official site: [faktorial.ai](https://faktorial.ai)
- Contact: [Run a pilot](https://faktorial.ai/#run-a-pilot) or email [info@asynkron.se](mailto:info@asynkron.se)
:::

## What Faktorial provides

- **GitHub-native delivery flow.** Issues, comments, pull requests, branches, and workflow results remain the source of truth while Faktorial coordinates the work around them.
- **Controlled autonomy.** Teams choose labels, branch targets, quality gates, approval points, and whether the system should stop at a review-ready PR or continue further under configured rules.
- **Traceable stages.** Work moves through investigation, build, verification, deploy, and learning stages so every job has explicit boundaries and evidence.
- **Learning loop.** CI friction, repeated retries, and useful decisions become agent rules, ADRs, or follow-up tasks instead of disappearing after a merge.

## Current status

Faktorial is presented publicly as a pilot or managed-service offering. The public site describes both a team-operated mode and a Faktorial Managed mode where Asynkron operates the delivery loop with senior engineering supervision.
